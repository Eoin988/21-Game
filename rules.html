21 for Fun

Rules

  1. The winner must either reach a total of 21 or acheive the closest to 21.
  2. If the dealer has 21 and the player doesn't, the player automatically loses.
  3. If the player has 21 and the dealer doesn't, the player automatically wins.
  4. If both the player and dealer have the same total, then it's a push.
  5. If neither side has 21, then each player plays out his hand, one at a time.

Player Controls

  1. Hit Me!: Take another card.
  2. Hold: Take no card on this round.
  3. Fold: Surrender deal
  4. Play: Check for 21
